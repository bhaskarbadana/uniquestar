ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This project aims to develop a user-friendly, centralized web platform that allows users to access various popular services such as online shopping, food delivery, movie streaming, anime content, and gaming from a single unified interface. Rather than switching between multiple websites or apps, users can conveniently launch their favorite platforms or search for content from one place.


Shopping Section :- Direct search and access to Flipkart, Amazon, etc.
Food Delivery Section :- Quick links to Swiggy, Zomato, with search integration.
Movie & Streaming Section :- YouTube-based search for movies and trailers.Google-based IMDb search for film info.
Anime Section :- Links to anime streaming platforms (like Crunchyroll, 9anime).Genre-based filtering (optional).
Gaming Section :- Links to gaming portals (e.g., Steam, Epic Games, or online games).News and release updates (optional future scope).
